How to Export from Showdown

1
Open Teambuilder
Go to play.pokemonshowdown.com/teambuilder and open or build your team.
2
Click "Export"
Click the Export button on your team. This copies the Showdown format text to your clipboard.
3
Paste Here
Click Paste from Clipboard or press Ctrl+V in the text area, then hit Parse Team.
4
Import & Build
Review the parsed Pokémon, then click Import to Card Builder to load them into your card.

Showdown Format Reference

Each Pokémon block looks like this:
Garchomp (M) @ Rocky Helmet Ability: Rough Skin Tera Type: Dragon EVs: 252 HP / 164 Def / 92 Spe Impish Nature IVs: 0 Atk - Stealth Rock - Earthquake - Dragon Tail - Fire Blast
Nickname support: Nickname (Species) @ Item
Shiny support: Shiny: Yes line
Gender: (M) or (F) after name
Separate Pokémon with a blank line
